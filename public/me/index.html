how I work

principle 01 Consolidate One repo, one worker, one source of truth. Fewer moving parts means fewer things that break silently.
principle 02 Build to Last Every infrastructure choice accounts for the long arc. Today's placeholder is tomorrow's Khem module. I design for the system I'm building toward.
principle 03 Own the Stack I prefer to understand the systems I use. When something doesn't exist or doesn't fit well, I build it, that's how Khem started.
principle 04 Ship Early Building in public, iterating in the open. Done is better than perfect, the site is live, the language is live, the garden is growing.
